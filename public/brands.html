Brands

    Nike

    Nike, Inc. is an American multinational corporation that is engaged in the design, development, manufacturing, and worldwide marketing and sales of footwear, apparel, equipment, accessories, and services. Nike is a long-standing staple in everyday clothing and functional clothing which makes it a suitable choice for buyers in search of clothing geared towards leisure or a specific purpose.

    RiotDivision

    Geared moreso towards the functionality and pushing the boundaries of its clothing in an acceptable sense. Riot Division is considerably utilitarian influenced in the sense that many of their pieces are focused on modularity and resistance to the elements under certain conditions.

    TheNorthFace

    The North Face, Inc. is an American outdoor product company specializing in outerwear, fleece, coats, shirts, footwear, and equipment such as backpacks, tents, and sleeping bags.Its clothing and equipment lines are catered towards college students, climbers, mountaineers, skiers, snowboarders, hikers, and endurance athletes.The company sponsors professional athletes from the worlds of running, climbing, skiing and snowboarding.

    Acronym

    A very high-end brand based in Berlin. ACRONYM clothing is considered one of the pioneers of techwear clothing due to their overall popularity and influence in terms of pushing techwear towards a reputable position within the fashion industry. ACRONYM clothing can run upwards of a thousand euros.
